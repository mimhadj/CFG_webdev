About Heybe Talent Management

Heybe Talent Management is on a mission to find and nurture talent in Sheffield and the Midlands, connecting local people with incredible opportunities around the globe. Sheffield resident Najma Heybe, hailing from Somalia, founded Heybe Talent Management after realising the challenges faced by less-advantaged people in Sheffield and Midlands looking to enter the world of film, theatre and music. Najma is a poet, director and screenplay writer, having recently written and directed the critically acclaimed "No Home for Nomads".



Meet the Team

Najma Heybe

Najma founded Heybe Talent Management after seeing talented youth in Sheffield go unseen. Najma aspires to connect people in the Midlands with the biggest names in film, theatre and music.

Muna Dalla

Muna is a former child actor and knows how to nurture talent from its inception. Muna is based in the Middle East and has built a sturdy network of connections seeking unique talent from the Midlands.

Maimuna Irmal

Maimuna wears many hats - you name it, she'll find a way to do it! Maimuna has a background in music and can spot potential in artists and musical theatre actors in the early stages of their careers.
